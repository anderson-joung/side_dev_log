# Room Background Prompts

## Common Style Prefix

Use this prefix for all room prompts:

```text
Polished full-screen 16:9 isometric game scene background for a Korean industrial survival tycoon game. Bright, white-friendly, readable UI-safe composition. Cute but tired cat, dog, and owl manufacturing workers. Korean small-to-mid-size manufacturing company. Clean soft shading, detailed but not cluttered, black-comedy survival pressure, industrial color palette, no existing IP, no logos, no watermark. Leave empty space near the left and right edges for UI overlays.
```

## 현장 / MCT CNC Machine Shop

```text
Polished full-screen 16:9 isometric game scene background for a Korean industrial survival tycoon game. Bright, white-friendly, readable UI-safe composition. Show an MCT/CNC machining shop inside a Korean small-to-mid-size manufacturing company. Include at least two large machines, one MCT and one CNC, plus a compact laser or secondary machine, parts trays, tool racks, safety floor markings, crates of machined parts, and a neat but busy industrial layout. Include cute anthropomorphic workers: one cat operating the MCT, one tired dog pushing a cart of machined parts, and one owl supervisor with a clipboard. Tone: hardworking, slightly tired, charming, black comedy. No text labels, no UI, no watermark.
```

## 조립실 / SMT Screen Printer Assembly Room

```text
Polished full-screen 16:9 isometric game scene background for a Korean industrial survival tycoon game. Bright, white-friendly, readable UI-safe composition. Show an SMT screen printer final assembly room. The room should clearly show an organized parts-and-assembly workflow for 36 machines total in 3 batches of 12 each. Divide the room into three zones from left to right: Zone 1 frame assembly, Zone 2 internal assembly and PLC wiring, Zone 3 idle run and testing. Show partially assembled SMT screen printer units, sorted parts bins, shelves, carts, PLC cabinets, wiring tools, test benches, jigs, and QC fixtures. Include cute animal workers: cat assembling a frame, dog carrying parts or wiring, owl checking a clipboard or monitoring a test. Productive and satisfying to watch. No text labels, no UI, no watermark.
```

## 사무실 / Cashflow War Room

```text
Polished full-screen 16:9 isometric game scene background for a Korean industrial survival tycoon game. Bright, white-friendly, readable UI-safe composition. Show a Korean manufacturing company office under pressure. Desks, computers, calculators, invoices, binders, AR/AP paperwork, coffee mugs, filing trays, and a window view toward a Korean industrial district. The room should feel like the cashflow war room: tidy enough to function, but visibly burdened by stacks of documents and responsibility. Cute animal staff: cat manager reviewing numbers, dog accountant tired but working through paperwork, owl handling reports or CS. Subtle black-comedy touches: instant coffee, late-night snacks, overstacked paperwork. No text labels, no UI, no watermark.
```

## 설계실 / Engineering Design Room

```text
Polished full-screen 16:9 isometric game scene background for a Korean industrial survival tycoon game. Bright, white-friendly, readable UI-safe composition. Show an engineering design room inside the same Korean manufacturing company.
```
